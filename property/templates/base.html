About Me

Hi, I'm Carlos Musk...

Hi there! My name is Carlos Kosala, I am the owner and operator of CK Real Estate. I obtained my Real Estate License shortly after completing my undergraduate degree in Business Administration and became a successful real estate agent with a well-respected brokerage in the Omaha area.... However, I wanted to take control of my own business and provide a more personalized approach to serving my clients, so I obtained my Broker's License and started my own real estate agency. While I enjoy the independence of running my own business, I also recognize the importance of certain services that my former brokerage provided, such as establishing an online presence to support my business. I am excited to continue growing and expanding CK Real Estate and providing top-notch service to all of my clients. Thank you for taking the time to get to know me and my business!
